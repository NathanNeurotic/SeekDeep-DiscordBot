HOW IT WORKS

  1. Pick a voice on the right and hit download.
  2. SeekDeep fetches the Piper model (~60–110 MB) and switches to it automatically — no restart.
  3. Type text in the middle and press Speak to hear it right here.
Downloading a voice also writes SEEKDEEP_TTS_PIPER_VOICE + SEEKDEEP_FEATURE_TTS_VOICE=on to your .env, so it survives a restart.
