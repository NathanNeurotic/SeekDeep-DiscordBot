EDIT-IN-PLACE · shared templates

▸ Editing a shared template locally:
  Now live. When you re-share an edited template:
    • if the bot still owns the original embed (≤ 14 days),
      edit-in-place via message.edit() — same channel post, new body, “edited” marker.
    • otherwise tombstone the original (strikethrough title + footer note)
      and post a fresh embed; importers keep their copy regardless.

▸ Deleting a shared template locally:
  same — keep the channel post for archival, just tombstone it.
  Users who already imported keep their copy.

▸ Cross-server sharing:
  intentionally NOT supported. Discord is the storage, and the channel
  scope is the community boundary.
