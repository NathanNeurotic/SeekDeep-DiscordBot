EDGE CASES · spec'd, awaiting backend

▸ Editing a shared template locally:
  the shared embed in #prompts goes stale (no live link to the local file).
  Mirror the archive pattern → edit-in-place via embed.edit() when the
  bot still owns the message; otherwise post a new embed and tombstone
  the old one with a strikethrough title + footer note.

▸ Deleting a shared template locally:
  same — keep the channel post for archival, just tombstone it.
  Users who already imported keep their copy.

▸ Cross-server sharing:
  intentionally NOT supported. Discord is the storage, and the channel
  scope is the community boundary.
