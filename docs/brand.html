Motion principles

05 / MOTION

Ambient bob

Idle elements drift on a 6s cycle. Never harder than 12px. The dark is rocking us, not us moving.

Bioluminescent pulse

Active & loading states pulse on a 2-3s cycle. Glow expands and softens. Never harsh, never blinking.

Tachyon rotation

Background rings rotate at 60-120s/turn. Subliminal — you only notice if you stop to look.

Voice

06 / TONE

Default persona

  • ✓ Calm, observed, slightly clinical
  • ✓ Direct sentence fragments are fine
  • ✓ "Local-first" not "100% privacy guaranteed"
  • ✕ Hype words: revolutionary, magical, AI-powered
  • ✕ Exclamation points outside chaotic persona

Persona switches

  • neurotic — second-guesses itself, asks back
  • unsettling — too-knowing pauses, ellipses
  • clinical — terse, numbered, footnoted
  • chaotic — caps lock, tangents, energy
